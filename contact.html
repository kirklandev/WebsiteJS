Contact Me

If you have any questions, feel free to email me at kirklandev@gmail.com. I'd be happy to help with anything I can, but my specialty is in fixed-wing FPV. This includes both conventional planes and flying wings, with FPV systems onboard. I would call myself an intermediate quadcopter pilot, as I've had multiple miniquads and a few larger 450 quadcopters in the past.

About me

My name is Evan Kirkland, and I am very passionate about aviation. I've been interested in flight for as long as I can remember; this has led to me pursuing remote-controlled aircraft as a hobby, one that I spend the majority of my free time doing. This ranges from fixed-wing aircraft to multirotors and drones. Specifically, I am interested with first-person view (FPV) flight, which involves a vehicle carrying a camera and live-streaming the footage back to me on the ground. I use this footage to pilot the vehicle, which gives me the sense of actually being on the aircraft.
